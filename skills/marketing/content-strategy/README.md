# Content Strategy

Plan content aligned with user personas and journey stages. Content strategy bridges the gap between knowing who your users are and delivering the right message at the right time.

## What's Inside

- Content Mapping — map journey stages to content goals and types
- Content Audit Template — assess existing content before creating new
- Editorial Calendar Template — plan content on a rolling 4-week cycle
- Content Types Guide — effort, impact, and shelf life for each format
- SEO Integration — align keyword strategy with personas and search intent
- Content Quality Checklist — pre-publish validation
- Content Repurposing — maximize every piece across formats and channels
- Measurement — KPIs and benchmarks by content type

## When to Use

- Planning a content strategy or editorial calendar
- Creating content aligned with user personas
- Auditing existing content for gaps and quality
- Mapping content to buyer journey stages
- Building a content repurposing system
- Aligning marketing/docs content with user journeys
