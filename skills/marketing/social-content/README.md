# Social Content

Create, schedule, and optimize social media content for LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook. Covers content creation, repurposing, and platform-specific strategies.

## What's Inside

- Platform Quick Reference — best formats, frequency, and key formats per platform
- Content Pillars Framework — build content around 3-5 balanced pillars
- Hook Formulas — curiosity, story, value, and contrarian hooks
- Content Repurposing System — turn one piece into many across platforms
- Content Calendar Structure — weekly planning template and batching strategy
- Engagement Strategy — daily routine, quality comments, community building
- Analytics & Optimization — metrics that matter and optimization actions
- Reverse Engineering Viral Content — analyze and codify what works
- Reference: Platforms — detailed platform strategies
- Reference: Post Templates — templates and additional hooks
- Reference: Reverse Engineering — complete viral content analysis framework

## When to Use

- Creating social media content for any major platform
- Building a social content calendar or batching workflow
- Repurposing existing content for social channels
- Improving engagement rates or growing audience
- Developing a platform-specific social strategy
- Writing LinkedIn posts, Twitter threads, or Instagram carousels
