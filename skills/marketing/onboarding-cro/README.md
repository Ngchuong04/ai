# Onboarding CRO

Optimize post-signup onboarding, user activation, first-run experience, and time-to-value. Help users reach their "aha moment" as quickly as possible and establish habits that lead to long-term retention.

## What's Inside

- Defining Activation — find your aha moment and activation metrics
- Onboarding Flow Design — immediate post-signup, checklists, empty states
- Multi-Channel Onboarding — email + in-app coordination and trigger-based sequences
- Handling Stalled Users — detection and re-engagement tactics
- Common Patterns by Product Type — B2B SaaS, Marketplace, Mobile App, Content Platform
- Measurement — activation rate, time to activation, funnel analysis
- Experiment Ideas — flow simplification, motivation mechanics, personalization
- Reference: Experiments — comprehensive A/B test ideas

## When to Use

- Optimizing post-signup onboarding or user activation
- Designing first-run experiences or empty states
- Improving activation rates or time-to-value
- Building onboarding checklists or setup wizards
- Reducing drop-off in onboarding funnels
- Planning onboarding email sequences
