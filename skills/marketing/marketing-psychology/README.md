# Marketing Psychology

70+ mental models and psychological principles applied to marketing and conversion. Includes a searchable database with CSV data and lookup by challenge.

## What's Inside

- Quick lookup by challenge — low conversions, price objections, building trust, urgency, retention, decision paralysis, onboarding
- Foundational Thinking models — First Principles, JTBD, Inversion, Pareto, Theory of Constraints
- Understanding Buyers — Availability Heuristic, Confirmation Bias, Sunk Cost, Endowment Effect, IKEA Effect
- Influencing Behavior — Reciprocity, Authority, Scarcity, Anchoring, Decoy Effect, Framing
- Pricing Psychology — Charm Pricing, Rule of 100, Good-Better-Best, Mental Accounting
- Design & Delivery — Hick's Law, AIDA, Nudge Theory, BJ Fogg Behavior Model
- Growth & Scaling — Feedback Loops, Network Effects, Flywheel Effect, Switching Costs
- Ethical Guidelines — genuine scarcity, truthful framing, respect autonomy

## When to Use

- Applying psychology to marketing or conversion optimization
- Working with cognitive biases, persuasion, or behavioral science
- Understanding "why people buy" or consumer decision-making
- Improving pricing, onboarding, or retention through psychological principles
