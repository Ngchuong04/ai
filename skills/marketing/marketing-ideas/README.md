# Marketing Ideas

Library of 139 proven SaaS marketing tactics with implementation guidance. Searchable by category, stage, budget, and timeline.

## What's Inside

- 139 marketing ideas organized across 17 categories
- Searchable database with CSV data and Python search script
- Filtering by stage (pre-launch, early, growth, scale)
- Filtering by budget (free, low, medium, high)
- Filtering by timeline (quick wins, medium-term, long-term)
- Top ideas by use case — leads fast, building authority, low budget, PLG, enterprise
- Workflow for recommending and deep-diving on selected ideas

## When to Use

- Need marketing ideas or growth strategies
- Looking for ways to promote a product or service
- Planning marketing tactics for a specific stage or budget
- Asking "how to market," "marketing tactics," or "ways to promote"
- Exploring SaaS marketing playbooks
